title: Introduction
description: What Tollway is and why it exists
---

Tollway sits above Stellar's two agent-payment primitives, x402 and MPP, and gives a service one coherent way to charge agents and an agent one coherent way to pay.

It routes each interaction to the right settlement model, runs the MPP channel lifecycle, enforces spending policy, normalizes every payment into one signed receipt, reconciles off-chain commitments against on-chain settlement, and exposes observability across all of it.

Tollway is not a facilitator and not a base SDK. It composes `@x402/stellar` and `@stellar/mpp`, and it never custodies funds.
