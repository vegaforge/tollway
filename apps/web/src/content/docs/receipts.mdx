---
title: Receipts
description: The canonical payment artifact shared across settlement models
---

Tollway normalizes x402 settlements, MPP charge settlements, and MPP channel
commitments into one receipt format. That gives accounting, analytics, and
dispute tooling one shape to consume.

Read the source design section: [One receipt format](https://github.com/vegaforge/tollway/blob/main/docs/design.md#one-receipt-format).

## Fields

A receipt records:

- Counterparties.
- Amount and asset.
- URL or resource id.
- Settlement model.
- Settlement reference.
- Timestamp.
- Tollway signature.

## Settlement references

For x402 and MPP charge, the settlement reference is a Stellar transaction hash.
For MPP channel mode, it is the channel id plus the cumulative commitment
sequence.

## Why signatures matter

Signed receipts provide a stable artifact for exports, dashboards, and dispute
evidence. Hosted deployments should rotate receipt signing keys safely and store
which key signed each receipt.
